Tips

  • Extraterritorial privacy (EU/UK) follows the data subject, not just your HQ.
  • HIPAA applies only to covered entities/business associates handling PHI.
  • EU AI Act = risk tiered; HR/credit/education/biometrics/essential services are typically high‑risk.
  • US = sectoral: FTC, EEOC, CFPB, FDA, plus state privacy & local audit laws (e.g., NYC y/n).
  • Generative & recommender systems trigger special platform/content rules in many regions.

Helpful frameworks (not laws)

  • NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0 — Govern/Map/Measure/Manage; excellent skeleton for AI risk program.
  • ISO/IEC 42001 (AI Management Systems) & ISO/IEC 23894 (AI Risk Management) — align your process; often recognized by regulators/procurement.
  • Model Cards, Data Sheets, Incident Playbooks — artifacts that make audits & regulator queries much easier.
